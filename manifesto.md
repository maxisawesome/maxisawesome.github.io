I had to write a goofy "manifesto" and ended up liking it a lot. incomplete and subject to change, lowercase on purpose

core tenets:

- understand the world
- love strongly
- friends, friends, friends
- honesty, levity

work and the field of ai:

- work hard, play hard
- be proud of what you do
- cautious optimism


### understand the world
- not much else to do
- its a big, wonderful world, and the only way to honor that is to try to understand it
- understanding the world does not reduce your awe of it

### love strongly
- I love love
- no need to shy away from adding more of it to the world
- more love is not a bad thing

### friends, friends, friends
- they are the greatest wealth you can possible obtain
- connection is what generates the previous two tenets


> You'll forget what you meant when you read what you said
> And yeah, we knew you were tired, but then
> Where are your friends tonight?
> Where are your friends tonight?

### honesty, levity, authenticity
- being honest is quite fun, actually
- being lighthearted is too and doesn’t reduce the quality of your work
- makes everything easier
- childlike wonder, a whimsical demeanor, and unadulterated joie de vivre

### work hard, play hard
- work to play and play to work
- don’t do anything if its not all the way

### be proud of what you do
- at the end of the day u need to be at peace w/ urself and what u do
- this is the way to do that

### cautious optimism
this is explicitly referring to AI, the progress of AI, and how to approach it
- shit’s crazy
- there are insane promises on all sides. take them and judge them for youself. we're likely gonna end up somewhere in the middle
- keep your cool and keep your head
